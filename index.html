Welcome to Blueprint Tracing Studio

Follow this simple 3-step tracing workflow to turn scanned images or Epson blueprints into clean, editable vector floor plans:

1

Upload Background Blueprint Underlay

Drag & drop or upload any PDF or blueprint image into the right panel. Adjust opacity to transparently trace on top of it.

2

Calibrate Real World Scale

Click Calibrate Scale. Draw a reference line across a known dimension on your blueprint, and input its size (e.g., a 10 ft doorway).

3

Trace, Decorate, & View in 3D!

Draw over the underlay with the continuous Wall and Window tools. Press Toggle 3D View to see your architectural space extrude into the third dimension!

Tip: Hold Spacebar/Middle mouse button anytime to PAN around your canvas, scroll your mouse wheel to ZOOM, and tap Delete/Backspace on targeted lines to erase elements!
