Section 1

Executive Summary

The Problem & Challenge

What Is The Actual Problem?

VIS wants to build an automated referral commission payout system similar to how Uber pays drivers.

Currently:

1

Recruiters/referrers bring students into the system.

2

Students make payments in multiple stages.

3

Recruiters earn commission/incentives at each stage.

The Company Does NOT Want:

  • ✕ Manual payout processing
  • ✕ Manual Excel calculations
  • ✕ Manual bank transfers
  • ✕ Finance teams approving payouts manually

Instead, The Company Wants:

  • ✓ Automatic commission calculation
  • ✓ Automated recruiter earnings
  • ✓ Instant or controlled payouts
  • ✓ Real-time recruiter earning visibility
  • ✓ Scalable infrastructure for hundreds/thousands of recruiters

In Simple Words:

"When a recruiter refers a student and that student makes payments, the recruiter should automatically receive commission into a digital earning system and withdraw the money similarly to how Uber drivers withdraw their earnings."

This System Is Essentially:

Fintech Payout System
Wallet Infrastructure
Commission Engine
Banking Automation System
Referral Tracking System

Final Recommendation

Best Model for VIS: Model 4 — Hybrid Wallet + Hold Period

Why Model 4 represents the optimal compromise between builder constraints and promoter excitement.

Good recruiter experience

Instant visibility establishes high trust. Promoters watch commission enter Pending status seconds after tuition deposits clear.

Fraud protection

Holding duration leaves ample time for automated activity checking algorithms to flag duplicate sign-ups or mock submissions.

Refund safety

If students cancel registration or trigger money chargebacks within the hold cycle, commission reverses smoothly within internal nodes.

Operational Controls

Enables better compliance, easier scalability, better finance control, and better bank approval probability.
